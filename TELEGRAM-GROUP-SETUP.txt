RESELLIX — Telegram GROUP setup (Thomas)
========================================

Your bot token is already in dev/.env. Switch alerts + commands to a group:

STEP 1 — BotFather (required for groups)
  Open @BotFather → send:  /setprivacy
  Choose your Resellix bot →  Disable
  (So the bot can read /commands in the group.)

STEP 2 — Create group
  - New Telegram group (e.g. "Resellix")
  - Add your friend
  - Add your bot (search its @username from BotFather)

STEP 3 — Wake the bot
  In the group chat send:  /start

STEP 4 — Save group chat id (on your PC)
  Open PowerShell in the project folder:

    cd dev\app
    .venv\Scripts\python.exe scripts\telegram_group_setup.py

  Follow prompts. It writes TELEGRAM_CHAT_ID to dev/.env
  (Group ids look like -1001234567890)

STEP 5 — Restart Resellix
  Close the app and run startwindows.bat again.
  In the group: /start  →  should reply with help.
  Deal alerts go to the group.

Optional: keep YOUR private chat too — script asks if you want
  group + private (comma-separated ids).

Multiple chats in .env example:
  TELEGRAM_CHAT_ID=-1001234567890,7917323723
